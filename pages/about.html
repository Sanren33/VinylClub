About Us

Founded in 2023, we have successfully organized more than 20 events, and we have managed to spread the vinyl culture in Sheridan. We are now partnering with 3 vinyl stores in the GTA as well as 1 online store to bring more benefits and experiences to our members.

Our mission is to preserve and pass on the classics of music while providing a platform for music lovers to connect and learn. Through various events and sharing music knowledge, we aim to promote the culture of vinyl records.
